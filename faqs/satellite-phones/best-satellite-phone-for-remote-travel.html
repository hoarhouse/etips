Remote Travel Communication Best Practices

Pre-Travel Preparation

  • Test Equipment: Thoroughly test satellite phone functionality before departure
  • Program Contacts: Pre-program emergency contacts, embassies, and local emergency services
  • Share Itinerary: Provide detailed travel plans to family and emergency contacts
  • Backup Power: Pack solar chargers, power banks, and backup batteries
  • Waterproof Storage: Use waterproof cases and dry bags for equipment protection

During Travel Communication Protocol

  • Regular Check-ins: Establish scheduled communication times with home base
  • Location Updates: Share GPS coordinates and status during dangerous or isolated segments
  • Weather Monitoring: Use satellite phone to receive weather updates and warnings
  • Local Emergency Numbers: Research and program local emergency service numbers
  • Embassy Contact: Know how to contact your country's embassy in emergencies

Emergency Communication Procedures

  1. Assess Situation: Determine severity and type of emergency
  2. Call Local Emergency Services: Use local emergency numbers when possible
  3. Contact Embassy: Notify embassy for serious situations involving foreign nationals
  4. Notify Family: Inform emergency contacts of situation and location
  5. Coordinate Rescue: Provide precise GPS coordinates and situational details
  6. Maintain Communication: Stay in contact with rescue services until help arrives

International Travel Considerations

Country-Specific Restrictions

  • Import Regulations: Some countries restrict satellite phone importation
  • Registration Requirements: Certain countries require satellite phone registration
  • Usage Restrictions: Some destinations ban satellite phone use in specific areas
  • Customs Declarations: Declare satellite phones at customs to avoid confiscation

Cultural and Practical Considerations

  • Local Customs: Respect local customs regarding communication device usage
  • Security Concerns: Satellite phones may attract unwanted attention in some regions
  • Language Barriers: Consider satellite phone support in local languages
  • Time Zone Planning: Plan communication times considering home base time zones
