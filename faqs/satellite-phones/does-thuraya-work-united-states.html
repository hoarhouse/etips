Does Thuraya Work in the United States?

No, Thuraya satellite phones do not work in the United States. Thuraya's coverage area is limited to Europe, the Middle East, Africa, Asia, and Australia. The continental United States, Canada, and South America are completely outside Thuraya's satellite footprint, making Thuraya devices useless for US emergency preparedness.
