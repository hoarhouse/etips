Why Satellite Phone Knowledge Can Save Lives

During the 2025 California wildfires and Hurricane Helene/Milton 2024, satellite phones were the only communication method working when cell towers burned or lost power. Understanding how to choose, use, and rely on satellite phones during emergencies isn't just helpful - it's potentially life-saving.

These FAQs answer every critical question about emergency satellite communication, from basic operation to cost analysis to real-world disaster scenarios. Whether you're preparing for wildfires, hurricanes, remote emergencies, or power outages, this knowledge helps you stay connected when everything else fails.
