Is Starlink Available in My Area?

Short Answer:

Starlink is available across most of North America, Europe, Australia, and expanding globally with service in 60+ countries as of 2025. Check exact availability at starlink.com using your address. Even if residential service shows "Waitlist," Starlink Roam (mobile) and Business plans often have immediate availability. For emergency preparedness, Roam service provides the most flexibility since it works anywhere in your continent with clear sky view, regardless of local capacity limits.

How to Check Starlink Availability

Determining Starlink availability for your specific location involves checking service maps, understanding capacity limitations, and choosing the right service tier for emergency preparedness needs.

Official Availability Check

  1. Visit starlink.com: Enter your exact service address
  2. Check status: Shows "Order Now" or "Waitlist"
  3. View timeline: Waitlist areas show estimated availability
  4. Try plus codes: Use Google Plus Codes for remote locations
  5. Check business tier: Often available when residential isn't
  6. Test Roam option: Mobile service bypasses local waitlists

Understanding Waitlists and Capacity

Starlink operates on a cell-based system where each geographic area has limited capacity based on satellite coverage and user density. This affects availability differently for various service tiers.

Why Waitlists Exist

  • Cell Capacity: Each area supports limited simultaneous users
  • Network Management: Prevents service degradation from oversubscription
  • Satellite Coverage: Some areas await additional satellite launches
  • Regulatory Approval: Some regions pending government authorization
  • Infrastructure Build: Ground stations being constructed

Infrastructure Requirements

Starlink requires certain infrastructure elements to provide service in any region:

  1. Satellite Coverage: Adequate satellites passing overhead
  2. Ground Stations: Gateway stations within 500 miles
  3. Regulatory Approval: Government authorization to operate
  4. Spectrum Allocation: Radio frequency permissions
  5. Import Permissions: Ability to ship hardware legally

Starlink Availability Questions

What if my exact address isn't recognized?
Use Google Plus Codes or GPS coordinates. Rural addresses often aren't in databases. You can also try a nearby address and update it after ordering.
Can I move my Starlink to a different address?
Yes, residential users can update their service address if the new location has availability. Roam users can use service anywhere in their coverage zone.
How long are typical waitlists?
Waitlists range from 2-12 months depending on location. Urban areas typically wait longer than rural areas due to higher demand.
Does bad weather affect availability?
Weather doesn't affect whether service is available in your area, but heavy rain or snow can temporarily interrupt active connections.
Can I pre-order for a future address?
Yes, you can pre-order with a $99 deposit for addresses on the waitlist. The deposit is fully refundable if you change your mind.
